Our Story

Multi-Flows.com was founded with a mission to advance the understanding and simulation of complex multiphase flow systems. Our team combines decades of experience in computational fluid dynamics with cutting-edge numerical methods.

We specialize in solving the most challenging two-phase flow problems across industries, from bubble dynamics in chemical reactors to cavitation in marine propulsion systems. Our expertise spans the full spectrum of multiphase phenomena including boiling, condensation, and phase change heat transfer.

Since our inception, we have collaborated with leading research institutions and Fortune 500 companies to deliver innovative solutions that push the boundaries of what's possible in multiphase flow simulation.
